Questions practices ask

Is this allowed under Google's rules?

Yes. We ask every patient the same way and never filter who gets invited, never pay for reviews, and never write them. That keeps you inside Google's review policies, which is exactly where a healthcare practice needs to be.

Do you write reviews for us?

No, and be wary of anyone who offers to. Fake reviews get filtered, and can get a profile suspended. Real patients write better reviews anyway; our job is making it effortless for them.

What do we have to do day to day?

One sentence at checkout: “if you were happy today, the card on the desk takes 30 seconds.” The cards and the link do the rest. Most practices spend zero extra minutes on it.

What about unhappy patients?

The rating page also offers a private “tell us directly” email option alongside the Google button, so you hear about problems first and can put them right. Everyone still gets the same invitation to review.

How fast do reviews come in?

It depends on your footfall, which is why we do not promise a number. What we do promise: if your first month brings in no new reviews at all, you do not pay for it.
