# Cosmic Nova

*The design philosophy behind NovaWebStudio — authored per the canvas-design
method, themed per theme-factory, with generative motion per algorithmic-art.*

---

## The movement

**Cosmic Nova** is the aesthetic of arrival by starlight. Every visit begins as a
journey: stars streak past, the destination scales up out of the dark, and the
traveler lands somewhere warm. Where most dark interfaces are cold, this one is
lit by a stellar event — the gold-white flare of a nova, cooling outward through
rose into the blue of deep space. It is playful the way space is playful:
enormous, a little magic, and impossible to look away from.

Space is not a backdrop here; it is the medium. The canvas is deep indigo-black,
alive with a field of stars at three depths that drift against each other as the
viewer moves — parallax as proof that the world has dimension. Content rides on
panes of glass that catch starlight at their edges. Nothing sits still, but
nothing fidgets: stars twinkle on slow cycles, a shooting star crosses perhaps
once a minute, and the journey between pages is a brief, joyful jump to
lightspeed. Motion always means travel — zooming toward, arriving at, orbiting
around — never decoration.

Type is the constellation the eye navigates by. The display voice is wide,
rounded, and unmistakable — letterforms that feel drawn with a compass, set huge
and tracked open like station signage on the far side of the galaxy. Against it,
a serif italic whispers the one human word in each headline. Labels are tiny,
spaced-out capitals — coordinates, not captions. The pairing must be executed
with painstaking, master-level care: monumental but never shouting, playful but
never cartoonish.

Color is thermodynamics. The nova itself burns **gold** — the color of every
call to action, every live signal, every moment of ignition. It cools through
**nebula rose** (the gradient's outer shell, the warm second voice) into
**stellar cyan** (constellations, connective tissue, fine lines of navigation).
The void stays disciplined indigo-black, and starlight text is near-white with a
violet breath. Gradients exist only as the nova's burst — gold into rose —
applied to the few things that are truly alive.

The rule beneath every rule: this must feel *fun to be inside* and effortless to
use. Interactions reward curiosity — the field leans toward the cursor, cards
tilt toward attention, numbers ignite as they enter view — but every button
reads instantly and every journey lands exactly where it promised. The result
should look labored over by someone at the absolute top of their field: countless
hours of calibration disguised as weightlessness.

## The theme (theme-factory format)

### Cosmic Nova

A warm-galaxy theme: a gold-to-rose stellar flare against deep indigo space,
navigated by cyan constellations. For AI products that want to feel like magic
without losing the plot.

**Color palette**

| Role | Name | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base | Deep Space | `#08070f` |
| Raised | Void Glass | `rgba(255,255,255,0.045)` + 1px `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)` edge |
| Primary flare | Nova Gold | `#ffb454` |
| Warm shell | Nebula Rose | `#f06595` |
| Navigation | Stellar Cyan | `#3bc9db` |
| Text | Starlight | `#f4f2ff` / 65% / 42% muted steps |
| App base (light context) | Paper | `#f8f7f3` with Ink `#12141d` |

**Typography**

- **Display / headers**: Unbounded — wide, rounded, cosmic; tracked open at small sizes
- **Editorial accent**: Instrument Serif *italic* — the one human word per headline
- **Body / UI**: Plus Jakarta Sans

**Rules of application**

1. The nova burst (gold → rose) marks only living things: CTAs, active states,
   captured leads, igniting numbers, the warp itself.
2. Stellar cyan is for constellations and connective lines — never buttons.
3. Every arrival is a journey: the first visit opens with a hyperspace warp;
   page-to-page navigation jumps to lightspeed; sections scale up into place.
4. Starfield parallax follows the cursor; stars twinkle slowly; shooting stars
   are rare enough to be lucky.
5. One serif-italic accent word per headline, set in the burst.
6. All motion honors `prefers-reduced-motion` with still, dense star charts.
7. The app (a working surface) stays on light Paper/Ink with the same type
   system — clients get the galaxy at the door and clarity at the desk.

**Chart palette** (dataviz-validated): `#3b5bdb` conversations · `#e8590c` leads
on light surfaces.

**Best used for**: AI platforms and creative studios that promise a little magic
— and deliver it on time.
