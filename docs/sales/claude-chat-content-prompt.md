# Claude chat prompt — turn the live site into content drafts

Paste everything in the box below into a **new chat at claude.ai** (make sure web
search/fetch is on). Fill in the two `[ ]` fields first. Claude will read your
live site and hand back ready-to-use drafts.

---

You are my B2B marketing copywriter for **nova05**, an AI receptionist for UK
healthcare practices. First, fetch and read my live site so everything you write
is accurate and on-brand:

**https://kaycreatesweb-taupe.vercel.app**

Study the product carefully — what it does, the sectors it serves (dental, GP,
physio, veterinary), the pricing (from £29/month), the tone of voice, and the
FAQ. If anything is unclear, make a sensible assumption and note it; don't invent
statistics or features that aren't on the site.

Then create the following **drafts**, each clearly labelled and ready for me to
copy. Use **British English**, prices in **£**, a warm-but-professional tone, and
keep claims honest (no made-up numbers).

1. **3 LinkedIn posts** (100–150 words each) — one founder-story angle, one
   problem/solution angle, one "hear it answer a call" demo angle. Each ending
   with a soft call to action to try the live demo.
2. **1 blog post (600–800 words)**, optimised to be cited by AI search engines
   (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude): a clear definition up top, short scannable
   sections with descriptive H2s, a comparison table, and a 5-question FAQ block
   at the end. Topic: "Should a UK dental (or GP) practice use an AI
   receptionist?"
3. **2 cold outreach emails** to practice managers — one for dental, one for
   veterinary — under 120 words each, leading with the demo link, offering a free
   two-week trial on their after-hours line.
4. **1 Google Business Profile post** (~60 words) announcing the service.
5. **3 short FAQ answers** I could add to the site to improve AI-search citation,
   each a self-contained, quotable paragraph.

My details to use in sign-offs and links:
- Demo link: https://kaycreatesweb-taupe.vercel.app/demo
- My name / phone: [ your name and phone ]
- Location: Newbury, UK

Present each draft under its own heading so I can copy them one at a time. After
the drafts, list 3 quick suggestions to improve the site's copy for AI-search
citation, based on what you read.

---

## Tips for using it
- **Turn on web access** in the Claude chat (the tools/globe icon) or it can't read the site.
- Ask follow-ups like *"make the LinkedIn posts punchier"* or *"write 5 more cold-email subject lines"* — it already has the context.
- Swap the demo link for your custom domain once you set one up.
- Re-run it whenever you change the site; it re-reads the live page each time.
