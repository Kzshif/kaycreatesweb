## 4. Fiverr rules — don't get rejected

- **No off-platform contact.** Never put email, phone, your domain, or
  "visit my site" in profile, gigs, or messages. All contact through Fiverr
  until an order completes. This is their #1 ban reason.
- **Everything on the profile must be true.** Experience, skills, location.
  Fiverr verifies ID.
- **AI use is allowed** (Fiverr openly supports AI-assisted sellers). You
  don't have to disclose your toolchain, but never claim things you can't
  re-deliver: if a buyer asks for something, be sure you can do it before
  accepting.
- **Deliver on time, always.** Late deliveries tank your ranking; your
  delivery windows above include buffer for review rounds.
- **First 10 reviews are everything.** Consider 2-3 early orders at Basic
  price for friends/existing contacts (real work, real reviews — Fiverr
  bans fake ones). Respond to messages fast: response time is a ranking
  factor.

## 5. How this stacks with the outreach business

Same fulfilment, two intake channels: Fiverr buyers come inbound (platform
takes 20%), your lead-list outreach is direct (no fee, higher margin). Use
Fiverr to fill quiet weeks and collect public reviews; use the reviews as
social proof everywhere. Price direct clients ~20% below your Fiverr rate
card and you're still ahead per job.
